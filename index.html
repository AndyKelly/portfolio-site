Andy Kelly

Bio

Hi, I'm Andy, a developer based in Dublin. My interest in my craft is huge. I'm a self-driven learner, I swallow books whole, to me a day where I haven't learnt something new is a day wasted. I value quality and stability in the product(s) I produce, I'm often pragmatic but contrarily I've got a hacker mindset. I love to tweak and experiment. I place enormous pride in my ability to inovate and come at and solve a problem from a new angle.

I like to think I'm a nice guy to be around, I thrive on the people I work with and nothing makes me prouder than being able to say I helped someone get their job done easier or that maybe they've learnt something new. On the flip side, the most important thing a company can offer me is a good team, with diverse and interesting people whom I can learn from.

At my current role I wear many hats. From building fault tolerant, scaleable services to maintaining databases to orchestrating deployments to building elegant and fun web apps.

.

If you think I can be of use to you or your team then please do get in touch. Thanks for stopping by!
